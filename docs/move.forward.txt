move forward <meters>

Move the robot forward by a specified distance in meters.

USAGE:
  move forward <meters>
  mov forward <meters>
  move fwd <meters>
  mov fwd <meters>

PARAMETERS:
  meters    Distance to move in meters (positive or negative)

EXAMPLES:
  move forward 1.5
  mov fwd 2.0
  mov fwd -0.5    # Move backward (negative distance)
