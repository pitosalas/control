move backward <meters>

Move the robot backward by a specified distance in meters.

USAGE:
  move backward <meters>
  mov backward <meters>
  move bak <meters>
  mov bak <meters>

PARAMETERS:
  meters    Distance to move in meters (positive or negative)

EXAMPLES:
  move backward 1.5
  mov bak 2.0
  mov bak -0.5    # Move forward (negative distance)
