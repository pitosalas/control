turn counterclockwise <degrees>

Turn the robot counterclockwise by a specified angle in degrees.

USAGE:
  turn counterclockwise <degrees>
  trn counterclockwise <degrees>
  turn ccw <degrees>
  trn ccw <degrees>

PARAMETERS:
  degrees    Angle to turn in degrees (positive or negative)

EXAMPLES:
  turn counterclockwise 90
  trn ccw 45
  trn ccw -90    # Turn clockwise (negative angle)
