turn clockwise <degrees>

Turn the robot clockwise by a specified angle in degrees.

USAGE:
  turn clockwise <degrees>
  trn clockwise <degrees>
  turn clk <degrees>
  trn clk <degrees>

PARAMETERS:
  degrees    Angle to turn in degrees (positive or negative)

EXAMPLES:
  turn clockwise 90
  trn clk 45
  trn clk -90    # Turn counterclockwise (negative angle)
